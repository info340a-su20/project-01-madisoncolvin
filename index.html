Welcome To Speak Out!

An Anonymous Protest Planning Application

Welcome to Speak Out's official website. Speak Out is an anonymous protest planning application. Our mission is to connect individuals who wish to spark a change to protests in their area. We hope to provide individuals the opportunity to speak out and make a difference in their community, the nation, and the world.

Speak Out is an application where users can anonymously post when and where protests are taking place in their area. Users can add protests they are interested in participating in to their built in calendar, allowing them to keep track of their protests and receive real-time updates. For large scale protests, the website will keep up-to-date information regarding road closures and known police involvement to make sure protestors stay safe while supporting their cause. This ensures that protesters are able to arrive at the protest safely and park outside of enclosed zones, know how to safely leave the protest once it is over, and ensures the safety of the individuals involved.

The proposed web application will keep up-to-date information of causes, such as GoFundMe’s, that are in need of donations if users wish to help the cause, but cannot make it out to the protest. The web application will also list petitions that have not yet reached their signee goal for those who are not able to financially contribute.

This app strives to connect individuals who would love to get more involved, but are struggling to find the information online. Speak out allows people to plan for safe and peaceful protests and aid them in their mission to spark change.

About Speak Out

The last few months have been a monumental and pivotal point in U.S. and world history. We are currently living through the world’s first modern pandemic and are in the midst of the Black Lives Matter movement, a fight against systemic racism in America and for police reform. The unjust murder of George Floyd by the Minniapolois Police Department has sparked protests across the world to combat police brutality, specifically against African-American’s in the United States. The Black Lives Matter movement has created a wave of large-scale protests across the nation in hopes to combat racial injustice and racism that still exists in our country.

Racial injustice is a prominent issue in our current society that needs to be addressed. Recent protests have been organized for individuals to gather and peacefully protest. Due to many individuals being unlawfully arrested for their involvement in these peaceful protests there has been a lack of information regarding protests posted online. When searching for locations and times of organized protests on the internet, social media and discussion boards it’s very difficult to find information regarding the subject. Many individuals watch protests play out on television, not hearing of them until they watch the protest in real-time on their local news station.

Local activist groups have tried to spread the word of protests via Twitter and Reddit, but despite this, there is still a lack of information available. Posts on Twitter or Reddit are often easily buried and hard for the inexperienced user to find. There needs to be a more accessible, user-friendly way to distribute this information. That’s why Speak Out was created.

We hope to provide a platform where users can spread important information regarding protests, increasing engagement and sparking change.
